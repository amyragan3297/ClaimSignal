For ClaimSignal, I would separate masking into four categories:

Claim Data Masking

Default for everyone except Master Admin:

* Claim Number → partially masked
    * Example: 800816754 → 8008*****
* Property Address → city/state or partial address only
* Policy Number → masked
* Email Addresses → masked
* Phone Numbers → masked

Homeowner Visibility

This is where I would make a deliberate decision.

You previously indicated you’re comfortable keeping homeowner names visible internally, but for exports and shared intelligence:

* Internal platform view: homeowner visible
* Playbook view: homeowner masked
* Carrier intelligence view: homeowner masked
* Adjuster intelligence view: homeowner masked
* Investor view: homeowner masked

Example:

Frederick Marshall

becomes

F. Marshall

Contractor-Side Masking

You already identified this.

Automatically mask or replace:

* Prior company names
* Internal contractor contacts
* Former affiliations
* Internal email addresses

Use:

* Contractor Contact A
* Field Representative
* Prior Contractor
* Internal Contact

instead of specific names.

Playbook Intelligence Masking

This is probably the most important.

When a claim enters the Playbook Engine:

Display:

* Carrier
* Adjuster
* Damage type
* Strategy
* Timeline
* Outcome

Hide:

* Homeowner name
* Full address
* Claim number
* Policy number
* Email
* Phone

Because the playbook is supposed to teach patterns, not reveal claim files.
