SECTION 25

NETWORK INTELLIGENCE ENGINE

PURPOSE

Create a platform-wide intelligence layer that identifies patterns across claims, carriers, adjusters, outcomes, weather events, escalations, and playbooks.

The Network Intelligence Engine should become the foundation of ClaimSignal’s long-term competitive advantage.

The more verified claim outcomes entered into the platform, the more valuable the intelligence becomes.

⸻

CORE OBJECTIVE

Identify recurring patterns that individual users may not notice.

Examples:

* Certain denial reasons occurring more frequently
* Certain adjusters appearing across multiple carriers
* Certain escalation paths producing higher approval rates
* Certain storm events producing unusually high denial rates
* Certain documentation types leading to higher success rates

⸻

PATTERN DETECTION

Track:

* Carrier patterns
* Adjuster patterns
* Denial patterns
* Approval patterns
* Escalation patterns
* Storm patterns
* Supplement patterns
* Documentation patterns
* Timeline patterns

Display:

Detected Network Patterns

Example:

Repairability disputes are increasing among Carrier X during the last 90 days.

Only display patterns supported by actual data.

⸻

CROSS-CLAIM RELATIONSHIPS

Identify:

* Shared adjusters
* Shared carriers
* Shared denial reasons
* Shared escalation paths
* Shared storm events
* Shared documentation outcomes

Display:

Related Intelligence Signals

⸻

OUTCOME CORRELATION ENGINE

Analyze:

* Which actions most often precede approval
* Which actions most often precede denial reversal
* Which evidence types correlate with successful outcomes
* Which escalation paths produce measurable results

Display:

Outcome Correlation Summary

Example:

Claims including repairability documentation achieved a higher approval rate than similar claims without repairability documentation.

Do not imply causation unless supported by sufficient evidence.

⸻

TREND ANALYSIS

Track:

* 30-Day Trends
* 90-Day Trends
* 180-Day Trends
* 365-Day Trends

Examples:

* Increasing denial activity
* Increasing approval activity
* Rising supplement resistance
* Increasing reinspection requests
* Growing use of certain escalation paths

⸻

INTELLIGENCE CONFIDENCE

Every pattern must display:

* Sample Size
* Confidence Level
* Date Range

If insufficient evidence exists:

Display:

Insufficient evidence to establish a reliable pattern.

⸻

NETWORK SIGNALS

Generate:

* Emerging Carrier Signals
* Emerging Adjuster Signals
* Emerging Documentation Signals
* Emerging Escalation Signals

Only from actual historical data.

⸻

PRIVACY AND MASKING

Network Intelligence must never expose:

* Homeowner names
* Addresses
* Policy numbers
* Claim numbers
* Personal contact information

Use aggregate intelligence only.

⸻

AUDIT LOGGING

Track:

* Pattern generated
* Trend viewed
* Correlation report viewed
* Network intelligence report generated

⸻

FINAL VALIDATION

Verify:

1. Patterns use actual historical data.
2. Confidence levels are displayed.
3. Sample sizes are displayed.
4. No protected information is exposed.
5. Network reports remain aggregate-only.
6. Trend calculations are accurate.
7. Correlation analysis remains evidence-based.
8. Audit logs capture activity.
9. Existing permissions remain intact.
10. No fabricated intelligence is displayed.

SUCCESS CONDITION

ClaimSignal develops a growing intelligence network that identifies patterns, trends, and outcome correlations across carriers, adjusters, claims, storms, and escalations while preserving privacy and data integrity.
