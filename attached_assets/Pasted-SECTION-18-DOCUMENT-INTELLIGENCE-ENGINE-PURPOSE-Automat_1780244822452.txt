SECTION 18

DOCUMENT INTELLIGENCE ENGINE

PURPOSE

Automatically extract intelligence from uploaded claim documents and populate ClaimSignal fields.

The user should not have to manually enter information already present in documents.

SUPPORTED DOCUMENT TYPES

* Carrier estimates
* Xactimate estimates
* Denial letters
* Coverage letters
* Reservation of rights letters
* Engineer reports
* Inspection reports
* Invoices
* Contracts
* ITEL reports
* Photographs
* Email exports
* PDF claim packets
* Audio transcripts

⸻

AUTOMATIC EXTRACTION

When a document is uploaded, extract:

Claim Information

* Claim number
* Date of loss
* Carrier
* Policy number
* Property address
* Claim status

Financial Information

* RCV
* ACV
* Recoverable depreciation
* Deductible
* Net payment
* O&P
* Supplements
* Permit costs

Adjuster Information

* Adjuster name
* Adjuster role
* Supervisor name
* Independent adjusting firm
* Carrier representative

Property Information

* Roof type
* Number of stories
* Steep designation
* Detached structures
* Interior damages
* Additional structures

⸻

DENIAL DETECTION

Automatically detect:

* Full denial
* Partial denial
* Coverage limitation
* Matching dispute
* Repairability dispute
* Wear and tear language
* Mechanical damage language
* Installation defect language
* Maintenance language

Display:

Detected Denial Reasons

Confidence Level

⸻

MISSING ITEM DETECTION

Compare uploaded estimates against ClaimSignal standards.

Flag possible omissions:

* Drip edge
* Starter
* Ridge cap
* Ridge vent
* Ice and water shield
* Valley liner
* Pipe jacks
* Flashing
* Permit
* Dumpster
* O&P
* Steep charges
* High charges
* Detachment items

Display:

Potential Missing Line Items

Confidence Level

⸻

TIMELINE EXTRACTION

Automatically identify:

* Inspection dates
* Reinspection dates
* Payment dates
* Denial dates
* Coverage dates
* Escalation dates

Create timeline entries automatically.

⸻

PLAYBOOK LINKING

After extraction:

Search historical claims.

Suggest:

* Similar carriers
* Similar adjusters
* Similar denial reasons
* Similar outcomes

Display:

Related Historical Playbooks

⸻

AUDIT LOGGING

Record:

* Document uploaded
* Document analyzed
* Data extracted
* Timeline generated
* Missing items detected
* Denial reason detected
* Playbook linked

⸻

SAFETY

Never overwrite user-entered data automatically.

Always show:

Review Suggested Data

Accept
Reject
Edit

before modifying claim records.

⸻

SUCCESS CONDITION

Users upload documents and ClaimSignal automatically extracts structured intelligence, identifies denial patterns, flags possible omissions, builds timelines, and recommends relevant historical playbooks while preserving user control.
