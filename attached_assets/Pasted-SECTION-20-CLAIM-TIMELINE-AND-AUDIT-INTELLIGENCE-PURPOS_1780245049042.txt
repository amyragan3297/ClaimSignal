SECTION 20

CLAIM TIMELINE AND AUDIT INTELLIGENCE

PURPOSE

Create a complete chronological history of every claim activity, document, communication, decision, escalation, and outcome.

The timeline should answer:

What happened?

When did it happen?

Who did it?

What changed?

What evidence supports it?

The timeline should become the system of record for claim activity.

⸻

TIMELINE EVENTS

Automatically create timeline entries for:

* Claim created
* Claim updated
* Document uploaded
* Document analyzed
* Adjuster linked
* Adjuster removed
* Carrier assigned
* Status changed
* Estimate uploaded
* Payment recorded
* Supplement submitted
* Denial received
* Coverage letter received
* Reinspection requested
* Reinspection completed
* Escalation initiated
* Escalation resolved
* Playbook linked
* Storm verification completed
* Outcome updated
* Claim closed

⸻

TIMELINE ENTRY FORMAT

Each timeline entry should contain:

Date:
Time:
Event Type:
User:
Source:
Description:

Example:

Date:
05/20/2025

Time:
10:14 AM

Event Type:
Denial Received

User:
Amy R.

Source:
Uploaded PDF

Description:
Carrier denial letter uploaded and analyzed. Repairability language detected.

⸻

AUDIT INTELLIGENCE

Every change made inside ClaimSignal should be auditable.

Track:

* Original value
* New value
* User making change
* Date and time
* Source of change
* Reason if provided

Example:

Field:
Claim Status

Previous:
Pending

Updated:
Denied

Changed By:
Amy R.

Timestamp:
05/20/2025 10:14 AM

⸻

SECURITY

Master Admin:
Full timeline and audit access.

Team Admin:
Team claim access.

Team Member:
Permitted claim access.

Individual:
Own claims only.

Executive:
Aggregate timeline metrics only.

Preserve masking rules for all non-master users.

⸻

FINAL VALIDATION

Verify:

1. Timeline events generate automatically.
2. Manual timeline entries can be added.
3. Audit records capture all changes.
4. Chain of custody is preserved.
5. Searches return accurate results.
6. No fictional events are created.
7. Timeline exports function correctly.
8. Role permissions are enforced.
9. Masking rules remain active.
10. Historical records cannot be silently altered.

SUCCESS CONDITION

ClaimSignal maintains a complete, searchable, defensible history of every claim, document, escalation, and outcome while preserving accountability, transparency, and audit integrity.
