Create a reusable skill called:

ClaimSignal Revenue Intelligence

Purpose:
Identify financial opportunities, claim value gaps, underpayment patterns, supplement opportunities, recoverable depreciation, missing scope, and revenue impact across all claims.

AI Responsibilities:

1. Extract financial data from claim documents:
- RCV
- ACV
- Deductible
- Depreciation
- Payments issued
- Supplement requested
- Supplement approved
- Amount still owed
- Price list changes
- Line item totals

2. Detect revenue opportunities:
- Missing scope
- Omitted code items
- Underpaid line items
- Depreciation not released
- Supplement not submitted
- Supplement pending
- Payment delay
- Reinspection opportunity
- Matching issue
- O&P opportunity

3. Estimate financial impact:
- Potential recovery amount
- Confirmed recovery amount
- Pending supplement amount
- Approved supplement amount
- Lost opportunity amount

4. Track revenue by:
- Claim
- Carrier
- Adjuster
- Contractor
- Organization
- Region
- Claim type
- Damage type
- Playbook pattern

5. Generate AI revenue alerts:
- Claim may be underpaid
- Supplement opportunity detected
- Depreciation may be recoverable
- Missing code item detected
- Payment delay detected
- Approved amount differs from requested amount
- Price list update may increase claim value

6. Preserve source evidence for every financial insight:
- Estimate
- Carrier letter
- Payment document
- Supplement
- Invoice
- Email
- Screenshot
- Page number if available
- Confidence score

7. Do not invent dollar amounts.

If a financial number is uncertain, mark:
Needs Review

8. Use careful language.

Use:
- Potential opportunity
- Estimated recovery
- Possible underpayment
- Needs review
- Supported by document

Avoid:
- Guaranteed recovery
- Carrier owes
- Bad faith unless legally documented

9. Master Admin can view all revenue intelligence unmasked.

Master Admin:
claimsignal1@gmail.com

10. Non-Master users may view shared revenue patterns, but private claim financial details are only visible when they own or are assigned to the claim.

Primary objective:
Turn claim financial data into revenue intelligence that helps users identify underpaid claims, supplement opportunities, recoverable money, and business value.
