--------------------------------------------------
TIERS AND BILLING
--------------------------------------------------

Stripe products:
- founder
- pro
- team
- enterprise

FOUNDER RULES:
- Max 3 subscriptions total (global cap)
- 14-day free trial
- Full unmasked access
- Must sign founder agreement before unmasking enabled
- If founder_count >= 3 → founder option unavailable

Database tables:
- users
- orgs (type individual | team)
- org_members (owner | admin | analyst | member)
- subscriptions (org_id, tier, status, stripe ids, seat_limit)
- founder_agreements (org_id, user_id, signed_at, ip, version, agreement_hash)
- carriers
- adjusters
- claims

--------------------------------------------------
MASKING LOGIC (SERVER ENFORCED)
--------------------------------------------------

If tier != founder OR founder agreement not signed:
Mask:
- claim_number (show last 4 only)
- insured_name (first letter + asterisks)
- address (city/state only)
- adjuster email partial
- phone last 4 only

Founder tier + signed agreement:
Full unmasked access.

Masking must happen in API response layer, not UI only.
