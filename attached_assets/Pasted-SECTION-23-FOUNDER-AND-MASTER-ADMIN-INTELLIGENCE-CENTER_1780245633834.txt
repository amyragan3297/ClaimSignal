SECTION 23

FOUNDER AND MASTER ADMIN INTELLIGENCE CENTER

PURPOSE

Provide a platform-wide intelligence command center available only to Master Admin users.

The Founder Intelligence Center should provide visibility into the entire ClaimSignal intelligence network while preserving tenant isolation for all non-admin users.

The purpose is to identify patterns, trends, opportunities, risks, carrier behavior, adjuster behavior, and platform growth signals.

⸻

MASTER ADMIN ONLY

Access Restricted To:

* Master Admin

No other user role may access this section.

⸻

PLATFORM OVERVIEW

Display:

Platform Intelligence Summary

* Total Claims
* Total Carriers
* Total Adjusters
* Total Playbooks
* Total Escalations
* Total Users
* Total Teams
* Total Uploaded Documents
* Total Timeline Events

Display growth trends over time.

⸻

CARRIER INTELLIGENCE LEADERBOARD

Display:

Top Carriers By:

* Denial Rate

* Escalation Rate

* State
* Date Range

FINAL VALIDATION

Verify:

1. Only Master Admin can access the Intelligence Center.
2. Tenant isolation remains intact.
3. Investor metrics contain no protected information.
4. Aggregated reporting is accurate.
5. Playbook effectiveness calculations use real outcomes.
6. Trend analysis uses actual historical data.
7. Audit logs record all activity.
8. Existing permissions remain unchanged.
9. Masking rules remain active.
10. No fabricated metrics are displayed.

SUCCESS CONDITION

ClaimSignal provides a Founder-level command center that reveals platform-wide intelligence, historical outcomes, trend analysis, and growth metrics while preserving privacy, security, and tenant isolation.
