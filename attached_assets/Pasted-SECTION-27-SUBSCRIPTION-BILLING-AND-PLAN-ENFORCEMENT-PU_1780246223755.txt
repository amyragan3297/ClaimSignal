SECTION 27

SUBSCRIPTION BILLING AND PLAN ENFORCEMENT

PURPOSE

Create the commercial subscription system for ClaimSignal so users can sign up, select a plan, pay through Stripe, and access features based on their subscription tier.

This section controls monetization, plan access, seat limits, billing status, and subscription enforcement.

⸻

SUBSCRIPTION PLANS

Create plan structure for:

FOUNDER PLAN

* Locked founder pricing
* Limited availability
* Early access features
* ClaimSignal intelligence access
* Playbook access
* Adjuster intelligence access
* Carrier intelligence access

INDIVIDUAL PLAN

* Single user
* Own claims only
* Claim intelligence dashboard
* Document intelligence
* Playbook search
* Adjuster and carrier intelligence based on permitted data

TEAM PLAN

* Team Admin
* Team Members
* Shared team claims
* Team intelligence dashboard
* Multi-user claim management
* Seat-based billing
* Additional seats billed separately

EXECUTIVE PLAN

* Read-only dashboards
* Aggregate reporting
* No claim editing
* No homeowner-level claim access unless specifically authorized

INVESTOR VIEWER

* Investor-safe reporting only
* Aggregate metrics only
* No protected claim data

⸻

STRIPE INTEGRATION

Integrate Stripe for:

* New subscription checkout
* Monthly billing
* Annual billing if enabled
* Payment method updates
* Failed payment handling
* Subscription cancellation
* Subscription renewal
* Plan upgrades
* Plan downgrades
* Seat quantity updates

⸻

BILLING STATUS

Track:

* Active
* Trialing if enabled
* Past due
* Canceled
* Incomplete
* Unpaid
* Payment failed

Do not grant paid feature access unless the subscription status allows access.

⸻

PLAN ENFORCEMENT

Restrict access by plan.

Examples:

Individual Plan:

* Own claims only
* No team management
* No investor dashboard
* No platform-wide intelligence center

Team Plan:

* Team claim access
* Team Admin controls
* Seat management
* Team reporting

Executive Plan:

* Read-only dashboards
* Aggregate reporting only

Investor Viewer:

* Investor-safe dashboard only

Master Admin:

* Full platform access

⸻

SEAT MANAGEMENT

For team accounts:

Track:

* Included seats
* Additional seats
* Active users
* Pending invites
* Removed users
* Billing quantity

Prevent users from exceeding plan seat limits unless billing quantity is updated.

⸻

UPGRADE AND DOWNGRADE FLOW

Allow users to:

* Upgrade from Individual to Team
* Add seats
* Remove seats
* Change billing cycle
* Cancel subscription
* Reactivate subscription

Downgrades should not delete data.

If features become unavailable after downgrade, preserve data but restrict access.

Display:

This feature is not included in your current plan.

⸻

FOUNDER ACCOUNT PROTECTION

Founder accounts should retain locked pricing unless manually changed by Master Admin.

Do not automatically remove founder pricing.

Founder status should be visible only to authorized roles.

⸻

BILLING PORTAL

Provide access to:

* Update payment method
* View invoices
* Download receipts
* Change plan if allowed
* Cancel subscription if allowed

⸻

ACCESS LOCKOUT RULES

If payment fails or subscription becomes inactive:

Allow user to:

* Log in
* View billing page
* Update payment method
* Contact support

Restrict:

* Claim creation
* New uploads
* AI Copilot
* Playbook search
* Intelligence dashboards

Do not delete claim data.

⸻

AUDIT LOGGING

Log:

* Subscription created
* Subscription updated
* Subscription canceled
* Payment failed
* Payment restored
* Plan upgraded
* Plan downgraded
* Seat added
* Seat removed
* Billing portal opened

⸻

SECURITY

Billing data must not expose protected payment details.

Do not store full card numbers.

Use Stripe-hosted secure billing tools where possible.

⸻

FINAL VALIDATION

Verify:

1. Users can select a plan.
2. Stripe checkout works.
3. Subscription status is saved.
4. Paid features are gated correctly.
5. Team seat limits work.
6. Founder pricing is preserved.
7. Failed payments restrict paid features but do not delete data.
8. Investor access remains aggregate-only.
9. Master Admin retains full access.
10. Audit logs capture billing events.

SUCCESS CONDITION

ClaimSignal supports real subscription billing, plan-based access control, team seat enforcement, founder pricing protection, and secure Stripe-powered monetization without exposing protected claim data or deleting user records.
