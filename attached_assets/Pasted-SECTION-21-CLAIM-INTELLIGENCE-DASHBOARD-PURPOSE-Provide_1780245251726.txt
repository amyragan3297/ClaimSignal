SECTION 21

CLAIM INTELLIGENCE DASHBOARD

PURPOSE

Provide a single-screen intelligence view showing claim health, risk factors, historical patterns, and recommended next actions.

The dashboard should answer:

What is happening?

What is at risk?

What should I focus on next?

What worked before in similar claims?

⸻

CLAIM HEALTH SCORE

Generate a Claim Health Score using available claim data.

Factors may include:

* Claim status
* Time since date of loss
* Denial history
* Escalation history
* Missing documents
* Adjuster history
* Carrier history
* Supplement status
* Timeline completeness

Display:

Claim Health Score

Excellent
Good
Moderate Risk
High Risk

Do not display a score if insufficient data exists.

Display:

Insufficient data for claim health scoring.

⸻

CLAIM RISK SIGNALS

Track:

* Prior denial
* Repeated denial
* Claim closure threat
* Delayed response
* Missing documentation
* Carrier dispute
* Repairability dispute
* Matching dispute
* Brittle test issue
* Coverage dispute
* Supplement resistance

Display:

Risk Signals Detected

With severity:

* Low
* Medium
* High

Never fabricate risk signals.

⸻

ADJUSTER SIGNAL SUMMARY

Display:

* Adjuster Name
* Linked Claims
* Denial Rate
* Overturn Rate
* Escalation Frequency
* Reinspection Frequency
* Data Confidence

If insufficient evidence:

Display:

Limited adjuster history available.

⸻

CARRIER SIGNAL SUMMARY

Display:

* Carrier
* Denial Rate
* Overturn Rate
* Escalation Frequency
* Common Denial Reasons
* Average Resolution Time
* Data Confidence

If insufficient evidence:

Display:

Limited carrier history available.

⸻

PLAYBOOK MATCHES

Automatically display:

Top Historical Matches

For each match show:

* Similarity Score
* Carrier
* Adjuster
* Initial Outcome
* Final Outcome
* Strategy Used

Only use actual historical data.

⸻

RECOMMENDED NEXT ACTIONS

Generate suggested actions based on:

* Current claim status
* Available documentation
* Historical outcomes
* Escalation history

Examples:

Suggested Next Actions

* Request reinspection
* Upload additional photos
* Submit repairability documentation
* Escalate to supervisor review
* Submit estimate comparison

Display:

Recommended based on historical claim patterns.

Do not present recommendations as legal advice.

⸻

DASHBOARD ALERTS

Display:

Critical Alerts

Examples:

* Missing denial letter
* Missing estimate
* Missing date of loss
* Missing carrier assignment
* Missing adjuster assignment
* Incomplete timeline
* Unresolved escalation

⸻

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Generate:

Claim Intelligence Summary

Example:

Claim currently shows elevated risk due to prior denial, repairability dispute, and delayed carrier response. Historical claims with similar characteristics achieved approval following reinspection and supplemental documentation.

This summary must be generated only from available data.

⸻

AUDIT LOGGING

Log:

* Dashboard viewed
* Risk score generated
* Playbook recommendation viewed
* Recommended action viewed
* Intelligence summary generated

⸻

SUCCESS CONDITION

ClaimSignal provides a single intelligence dashboard that transforms claim data, adjuster history, carrier history, playbooks, escalations, and timeline activity into actionable operational insight without fabricating information or exposing protected data.
