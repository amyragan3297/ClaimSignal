SECTION 22

CLAIMSIGNAL AI COPILOT

PURPOSE

Provide an intelligent claim assistant that can analyze claim data, answer questions, summarize claim activity, identify risks, recommend actions, and surface historical intelligence.

The AI Copilot should operate as a claim intelligence assistant, not as a legal advisor, public adjuster, attorney, or claim decision-maker.

⸻

CORE OBJECTIVE

Allow users to ask natural language questions about claims.

Examples:

* Why is this claim at risk?
* What should I do next?
* What documents are missing?
* Show similar successful claims.
* Summarize this denial letter.
* What escalation worked before?
* What is the carrier pattern?
* What is the adjuster history?
* Why was this claim denied?
* What evidence is missing?

⸻

CLAIM SUMMARY MODE

Generate a claim summary from available data.

Include:

* Carrier
* Adjuster
* Date of Loss
* Current Status
* Initial Outcome
* Current Outcome
* Timeline Summary
* Escalation History
* Financial Summary
* Open Risks
* Missing Information

Display:

Claim Intelligence Summary

Only use verified data.

Do not fabricate facts.

⸻

DOCUMENT ANALYSIS MODE

Allow users to upload:

* Denial letters
* Coverage letters
* Estimates
* Engineer reports
* Emails
* Inspection reports
* Invoices
* Claim packets

AI should identify:

* Denial reasons
* Missing documentation
* Missing line items
* Timeline events
* Escalation opportunities
* Related playbooks

Display confidence levels.

⸻

PLAYBOOK ADVISOR

When analyzing a claim:

Search historical outcomes.

Display:

Similar Claims Found

For each match:

* Carrier
* Adjuster
* Initial Outcome
* Final Outcome
* Escalation Used
* Strategy Used

Only use actual historical claim data.

⸻

NEXT ACTION ADVISOR

Provide suggested operational actions.

Examples:

* Request reinspection
* Upload additional evidence
* Submit estimate comparison
* Escalate to supervisor
* Add repairability documentation
* Add brittle test evidence

Display:

Suggested Based on Historical Claim Patterns

Do not present recommendations as legal advice.

⸻

ADJUSTER INTELLIGENCE MODE

Allow users to ask:

* What patterns exist for this adjuster?
* How often are denials overturned?
* What escalation paths have worked?

Display:

* Linked claims
* Denial trends
* Approval trends
* Escalation trends
* Data confidence

⸻

CARRIER INTELLIGENCE MODE

Allow users to ask:

* What patterns exist for this carrier?
* What denial reasons are most common?
* Which escalation paths work most often?

Display only actual historical data.

⸻

MISSING DATA DETECTION

Identify:

* Missing denial letters
* Missing estimates
* Missing adjusters
* Missing timeline events
* Missing financial data
* Missing weather verification

Display:

Recommended Data Collection Items

⸻

SECURITY

Master Admin:
Full visibility.

Team Admin:
Team visibility only.

Team Member:
Permitted claim visibility only.

Individual:
Own claims only.

Executive:
Aggregate intelligence only.

Respect all existing masking rules.

⸻

AUDIT LOGGING

Log:

* Copilot question submitted
* Copilot response generated
* Claim summary viewed
* Playbook recommendation viewed
* Document analyzed
* Intelligence report generated

⸻

AI DISCLOSURE

Display:

Responses are generated from available claim data, historical claim intelligence, and configured platform rules.

Recommendations are operational guidance only and are not legal advice, engineering opinions, or claim determinations.

⸻

SUCCESS CONDITION

Users can interact with ClaimSignal through natural language and receive claim-specific intelligence, historical pattern analysis, document insights, and operational recommendations grounded in actual claim data.
