Create a reusable skill called:

ClaimSignal Executive Intelligence

Purpose:
Convert claim, adjuster, carrier, playbook, timeline, supplement, payment, and document data into executive-level business intelligence.

AI Responsibilities:

1. Aggregate all claim data across the platform.

2. Track executive KPIs:
- Total claims
- Open claims
- Closed claims
- Denied claims
- Approved claims
- Reopened claims
- Overturned denials
- Supplement approvals
- Aging claims
- Claims needing action

3. Track financial KPIs:
- Total RCV
- Total ACV
- Total deductible amount
- Total depreciation
- Total payments issued
- Total supplement amount requested
- Total supplement amount approved
- Total recovered dollars
- Estimated revenue opportunity

4. Track carrier performance:
- Claims by carrier
- Denial rate
- Approval rate
- Reinspection approval rate
- Supplement approval rate
- Average response time
- Average payment release time
- Highest friction carriers

5. Track adjuster performance:
- Claims by adjuster
- Adjuster involvement
- Approval patterns
- Denial patterns
- Reinspection outcomes
- Supplement outcomes
- Average response time
- Highest friction adjusters

6. Track playbook performance:
- Most successful playbook patterns
- Denial overturn patterns
- Supplement approval patterns
- Code documentation patterns
- Reinspection success patterns
- Documentation strategies associated with approval

7. Generate AI executive summaries automatically.

Every dashboard refresh should produce:
- Top risks
- Top opportunities
- Claims needing attention
- Aging claims
- Claims missing documents
- Emerging carrier trends
- Emerging adjuster trends
- Emerging denial trends
- Emerging approval trends
- Recommended executive actions

8. Use careful language.

Use:
- Associated with
- Indicates
- Suggests
- Trending
- Needs review
- Requires action

Avoid:
- Guaranteed
- Caused
- Proves
- Bad faith unless legally documented

9. Preserve source evidence.

Every KPI and executive insight must connect back to:
- Claim IDs
- Source documents
- Timeline events
- Carrier records
- Adjuster records
- Playbook entries
- Confidence scores

10. Protect homeowner PII.

Executive-level summaries should not expose homeowner names, addresses, phone numbers, emails, or policy numbers unless the user is in Master Admin view and specifically opens the claim.

Primary objective:
Turn ClaimSignal into an executive decision-support platform that automatically identifies claim risks, opportunities, trends, revenue impact, and recommended next actions from the underlying claim intelligence.
